Offense Heroes

Offense Heroes are normally used for one thing: DAMAGE! Made to harrass and press for the point, Offense heroes have high mobility to help them get into and out of the fight. Examples of this are Genji's Swift Strike (Ability 2), Tracer's Blinks (Ability 1), and Reaper's Shadow Step (Ability 2).

A disadvantage that most offense heroes suffer from are a lower health pool. Despite having a lower health pool, most offense heroes have a way to either heal or avoid damage entirely. For example, Reaper's passive allows him to heal when he elimates someone, and his Wraith Form (Ability 1) not only makes him slightly faster, he also cannot take damage and is CC immune.
