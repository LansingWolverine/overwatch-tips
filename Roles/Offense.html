Offense Heroes

Offense Heroes are normally used for one thing: DAMAGE! Made to harrass and press for the point, Offense heroes have high mobility to help them get into and out of the fight. Examples of this are Genji's Swift Strike (2nd ability), Tracer's Blinks (1st Ability), and Reaper's Shadow Step (2nd Ability).

A disadvantage that most offense heroes suffer from are a lower health pool.
