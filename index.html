Welcome to my Guide!

Hello, I'm LansingWolverine or LANWolf if you've seen me play Overwatch or a few other games. I've been playing Overwatch since a couple days after it came out and frankly enjoyed it. Seeing how I put around 1.5k hours into TF2, I kinda knew I'd love it. As an Overwatch player I've wanted to improve how I play, so I went to the Internet to find techniques and strategies. Unfortunatly enough, there's been a lot of outdated info out there leading me to have a hard time figuring out which websites are relevent and which are not. This led me to attempt to make a website with (hopefully) information about current Overwatch stuff such as damage numbers, match-ups, and the likes of that. I'll try to update this website as frequently as I can, so don't be surprised if my website isn't updated the day after a big update. Thank you for checking this out and I hope you enjoy it.
